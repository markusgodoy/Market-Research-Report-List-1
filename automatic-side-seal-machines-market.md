<p><p>Automatic Side Seal Machines are packaging machines that seal products using a side seal method. There are three types of side seal machines: 2-side seal, 3-side seal, and 4-side seal machines. </p><p>1. 2-side seal machines seal products on two sides, creating a pouch-like packaging. They are commonly used for food and pharmaceutical products.</p><p>2. 3-side seal machines seal products on three sides, leaving one side open. They are suitable for packaging items like granular materials, powders, and liquids.</p><p>3. 4-side seal machines seal products on all four sides, resulting in a fully enclosed package. They are used for products that require maximum protection, such as electronics or medical devices.</p></p>
