<p><p>Spelter sockets are used in various industries for lifting and rigging applications. The market for spelter sockets can be divided into two types: open spelter sockets and closed spelter sockets. Open spelter sockets have an opening on one side, allowing for easy attachment and detachment of wire ropes. They are commonly used in applications where frequent rope changes are required. On the other hand, closed spelter sockets have a solid body with no opening, providing a stronger and more secure connection. They are typically used in heavy-duty applications that require maximum strength and load-bearing capacity.</p></p>
