<p><p>The Distributed Optical Sensing (DOS) System market includes three major types: Distributed Temperature Sensing (DTS), Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS), and Distributed Vibration Sensing (DVS). DTS measures temperature changes along a fiber optic cable, enabling real-time monitoring of industrial processes and infrastructure. DAS uses fiber optic cables to detect acoustic signals, providing applications such as detecting pipeline leaks or perimeter security. DVS utilizes fiber optic cables to measure vibrations, enabling the monitoring of structural health and detecting potential mechanical failures in various industries. These technologies offer efficient and accurate monitoring solutions for a range of applications.</p></p>
