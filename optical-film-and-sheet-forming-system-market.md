<p><p>The optical film and sheet forming system market includes both contact and contactless types. In the contact market, the film or sheet is directly molded or shaped by physical contact with a forming tool. This method is typically used for precision applications where close tolerances are required. In contrast, the contactless market uses techniques such as laser cutting or heat shaping to form the film or sheet without physical contact. This method is often preferred for delicate materials or when high throughput is needed.</p></p>
