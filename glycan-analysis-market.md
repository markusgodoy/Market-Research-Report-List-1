<p><p>Glycan analysis is a market that encompasses various techniques for studying the structure and composition of glycans (sugar molecules) in biological samples. Mass spectrometry is used to identify and quantify glycans, while High Performance Liquid Chromatography separates them for analysis. Capillary Electrophoresis measures glycans based on their charge, while Isoelectric Focusing separates them based on their isoelectric point. Polyacrylamide Gel Electrophoresis separates glycans by size and charge. These techniques play a crucial role in understanding the role of glycans in various biological processes and diseases.</p></p>
