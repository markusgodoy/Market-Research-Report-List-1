<p><p>Airlaid products are divided into different market types based on their production process and bonding materials. Latex Bonded Airlaid (LBAL) refers to products bonded together using latex, resulting in high absorbency and softness. Thermal Bonded Airlaid (TBAL) is made by using heat to bond the fibers, making them stronger and more durable. Multi Bonded Airlaid (MBAL) involves combining multiple bonding methods to achieve specific product properties. Hydrogen Bonded Airlaid (HBAL) uses hydrogen bonding to create a stronger and bulkier structure for enhanced absorbency and resilience.</p></p>
