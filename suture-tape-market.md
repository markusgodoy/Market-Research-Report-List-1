<p><p>Suture tapes are used in medical procedures to secure wound edges. Paper suture tapes are made from paper material and are often used in delicate surgeries due to their softness and ability to prevent damage to the skin. Conversely, synthetic suture tapes are manufactured using synthetic materials like nylon or polyester. They offer increased strength and durability, making them suitable for larger and more complex wounds. Both types of suture tapes provide effective wound closure options, but their specific attributes determine their applications in different surgical scenarios.</p></p>
