In the double mouths market, two transformers are employed, enabling the simultaneous transfer of signals between two networks. Lastly, the multiple mouths market involves the use of more than two transformers, facilitating the transfer of signals among multiple networks. These types differ in the number of transformers employed and the capability to transfer signals across different networks.</p></p>
