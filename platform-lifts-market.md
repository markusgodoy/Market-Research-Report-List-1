<p><p>Platform lifts are a type of assistive technology designed to provide accessibility and mobility solutions for individuals with disabilities or limited mobility. There are two main types of platform lifts available in the market - Vertical Platform Lifts (VPL) and Inclined Platform Lifts (IPL). VPLs are primarily used to vertically transport individuals or objects between different levels, whereas IPLs are inclined at an angle and are suitable for use on stairs or inclined surfaces. Both types aim to improve accessibility and independence for individuals with mobility challenges.</p></p>
