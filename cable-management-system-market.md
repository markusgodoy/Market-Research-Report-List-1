<p><p>The cable management system market comprises various types, including cable trays and ladders, cable conduits, cable trunking, cable connectors and glands, cable raceways, cable chains, and other types. Cable trays and ladders are used to support and manage cables in buildings or structures. Cable conduits are protective tubing that houses and organizes cables. Cable trunking provides a cover for cables and is commonly used in commercial buildings. Cable connectors and glands are used for secure cable connections. Cable raceways are enclosed channels that hide and protect cables. Cable chains are flexible chains that hold and guide cables in moving applications. Other types of cable management systems include cable cleats, cable tags, and cable ties.</p></p>
