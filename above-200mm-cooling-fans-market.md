<p><p>The above 200mm cooling fans market includes different types of fans such as centrifugal fans, axial fans, and others. Centrifugal fans are designed to create high-pressure airflow by drawing air into the fan housing and discharging it at a perpendicular angle. Axial fans, on the other hand, move air parallel to the axis of rotation and are commonly used for low-pressure applications. The "Others" category encompasses fans with unique features or designs that may not fit into the centrifugal or axial fan types.</p></p>
