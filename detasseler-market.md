Detasseling is typically performed manually by workers known as detasselers. These workers walk through the cornfields and remove the tassels by hand, ensuring that only female flowers are pollinated.</p><p>The Detasseler Market has witnessed significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue growing at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 5.7% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to several factors.
