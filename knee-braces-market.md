<p><ul><li>Prophylactic</li><li>Functional</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Knee braces are generally divided into two market types: prophylactic and functional. Prophylactic knee braces are designed to prevent or reduce the risk of knee injuries, commonly used by athletes participating in high-impact sports. On the other hand, functional knee braces are designed to provide support and stability to individuals who have already experienced a knee injury or have a knee condition. Functional braces are often recommended for post-injury rehabilitation or for individuals with chronic knee issues. Both types of knee braces aim to protect and support the knee joint during physical activity.</p></p>
