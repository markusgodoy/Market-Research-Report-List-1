<p><p>Bench top cup sealers are available in three market types: manual, semi-automatic, and automatic. Manual sealers require operators to manually place the cups and lift the sealing mechanism for each cup. Semi-automatic sealers automate the sealing process, but operators still need to place the cups. Automatic sealers fully automate the sealing process, from cup placement to sealing. These different market types allow customers to choose the level of automation they need based on their production volume and efficiency requirements.</p></p>
