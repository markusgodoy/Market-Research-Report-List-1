<p><p>The Sweetening Agent market is categorized into two types: High-intensity Sweetening Agents and Low-intensity Sweetening Agents. High-intensity sweeteners, such as aspartame and stevia, are substances that provide sweetness with a lower quantity compared to traditional sugar, making them a popular choice for calorie-conscious consumers. On the other hand, low-intensity sweeteners, like maltitol and sorbitol, are often used as sugar substitutes in food and beverages. They provide a milder sweetness and are suitable for individuals who prefer a less intense flavor.</p></p>
