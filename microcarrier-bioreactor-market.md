<p><strong>The Microcarrier Bioreactor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Microcarrier-based Anchorage-dependent Bioreactor</li><li>Suspension-based Anchorage-independent Bioreactor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Microcarrier bioreactors are used in cell culture processes for the production of various therapeutic proteins and vaccines. The microcarrier-based anchorage-dependent bioreactor is designed for anchorage-dependent cells, which require a substrate to attach and grow. In contrast, suspension-based anchorage-independent bioreactors support the growth of anchorage-independent cells, which do not require a substrate for attachment. Both types of bioreactors play a crucial role in the efficient expansion and production of cells for various applications in the biopharmaceutical industry.</p></p>
