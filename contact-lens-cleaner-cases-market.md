<p><p>The contact lens cleaner cases market consists of various types of cleaning technologies. Physical shock cleaning involves the use of mechanical force or agitation to remove dirt and debris from contact lenses. Ultrasonic cleaning uses high-frequency sound waves to generate microscopic bubbles that effectively clean the lenses. Protein electrophoresis cleaning utilizes an electrical current to remove protein deposits from the lenses. Each of these cleaning methods offers unique advantages in terms of efficiency and effectiveness in keeping contact lenses clean and safe for use.</p></p>
