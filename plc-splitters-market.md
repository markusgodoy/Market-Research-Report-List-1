Plug-In Type splitters are easy to install and convenient for replacement. Box Type splitters provide a protective enclosure for outdoor use. Lastly, Rack and Pallet Type splitters are designed for large-scale deployments in data centers or telecommunication hubs where multiple splitters are required.</p></p>
