<p><p>The rear-projection cubes market consists of two types: LED-lit and Laser-lit. LED-lit rear-projection cubes use light-emitting diodes to illuminate the display, providing vibrant colors and high brightness levels. They are energy-efficient and offer a long lifespan. On the other hand, Laser-lit rear-projection cubes use laser technology for illumination. They provide enhanced brightness, color accuracy, and a wider color gamut. Laser-lit cubes are known for their reliability and longer maintenance-free operation.
