The growing aging population and the increasing prevalence of conditions like myopia, hyperopia, and astigmatism are contributing to the market growth. Additionally, the increasing adoption of contact lenses and the advancements in lens technologies, such as the development of multifocal and toric lenses, are further fueling market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, the lenses market is witnessing a surge in demand in the healthcare industry due to the rising number of cataract surgeries and the increasing use of lenses in various diagnostic and surgical procedures.
