<p><ul><li>Continuous Level Monitoring</li><li>Point Level Monitoring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical level sensor market is segmented into two types: continuous level monitoring and point level monitoring. Continuous level monitoring sensors provide real-time measurements of fluid or material levels and are commonly used in tanks and vessels. They offer continuous data output, allowing for accurate and reliable monitoring. On the other hand, point level monitoring sensors detect specific levels or presence of materials. They function as switches and are used in applications where material levels need to be controlled or alerted at certain points. Both types cater to different monitoring requirements in various industries.</p></p>
