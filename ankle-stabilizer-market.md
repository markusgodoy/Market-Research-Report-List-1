<p><p>The ankle stabilizer market can be divided into two main types: lace-ups and hinged. Lace-ups are ankle braces that are secured using laces, providing adjustable compression and support. These are commonly used for mild to moderate ankle injuries and provide a snug fit. On the other hand, hinged ankle stabilizers have a built-in joint that allows for controlled movement while still offering stability. These are typically used for more severe ankle injuries or for individuals looking for extra flexibility without compromising support.</p></p>
