<p><p>The Border Security System market comprises three main types based on the surveillance medium used: underwater, ground, and aerial. Ground border security systems employ surveillance cameras, ground sensors, and radar systems to monitor and protect land-based borders. Aerial border security systems employ drones, aircraft, and balloons equipped with advanced sensors and cameras to provide real-time monitoring and intelligence gathering capabilities in the airspace above the borders.</p></p>
