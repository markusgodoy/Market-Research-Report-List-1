<p><p>Lab Informatics Market Types refer to various software systems used in laboratories to manage and analyze data. Laboratory Information Management Systems (LIMS) track samples, workflows, and results, while Electronic Lab Notebooks (ELN) digitize and centralize research notes. Scientific Data Management Systems (SDMS) focus on managing and organizing scientific data. Laboratory Execution Systems (LES) automate and monitor lab processes. Electronic Data Capture (EDC) and Clinical Data Management Systems (CDMS) handle clinical trial data. These systems aim to enhance efficiency, accuracy, and compliance in laboratory operations by streamlining data management and analysis processes.</p></p>
