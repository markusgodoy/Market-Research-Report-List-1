<p><p>The Waterproof TDS Meters market can be categorized into two types: High Range and Low Range. High Range TDS Meters are designed to measure total dissolved solids in water that have a higher concentration, typically above 1,000 parts per million (ppm). These meters are suitable for industrial applications and for monitoring water quality in heavily contaminated environments. On the other hand, Low Range TDS Meters are used to measure low concentrations of dissolved solids, usually below 1,000 ppm. They are commonly used for residential, commercial, and scientific purposes where water quality needs to be closely monitored.</p></p>
