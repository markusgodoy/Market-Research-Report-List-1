<p><strong>The IOT Smoke Detectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photoelectric</li><li>Ionization</li><li>Combination</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IoT smoke detectors market includes three main types: photoelectric, ionization, and combination detectors. Photoelectric detectors use a light source to detect smoke particles, making them perfect for slow-smoldering fires. Ionization detectors use ionized air to detect smoke, making them ideal for fast-flaming fires. Combination detectors combine both photoelectric and ionization technologies, offering a broader detection range for different types of fires. These detectors are part of the Internet of Things (IoT), allowing them to connect to a network and provide real-time alerts and remote monitoring capabilities.</p></p>
