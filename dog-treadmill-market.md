<p><p>The dog treadmill market offers two main types: electric and manual. Electric dog treadmills are powered by electricity and provide automated features, such as adjustable speed and incline settings. They are user-friendly and allow for a more controlled and customizable workout experience for dogs. On the other hand, manual dog treadmills are manually operated by the user, requiring physical effort to move the treadmill belt. They generally have fewer features but are more budget-friendly options for dog owners. Both types cater to different preferences and budget constraints in the market.</p></p>
