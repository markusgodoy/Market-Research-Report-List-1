<p><p>The Crawler Crusher market comprises various types, including Jaw Crusher, Double-roller Crusher, Impact Crusher, and Others. A Jaw Crusher consists of a fixed jaw and a moving jaw, which compresses large rocks or materials against each other. Double-roller Crusher features two parallel rollers that rotate in opposite directions, crushing materials between them. An Impact Crusher utilizes high-speed collisions between the hammer and materials to break them down. Lastly, the "Others" category includes different types of crushers, such as cone crushers or vertical shaft impactors.</p></p>
