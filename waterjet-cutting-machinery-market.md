<p><p>The Waterjet Cutting Machinery Market consists of two main types: Pressurized Water and Abrasive Mixture. Pressurized Water cutting machines use high-pressure water to slice through various materials, such as metals, plastics, and wood. On the other hand, Abrasive Mixture cutting machines combine high-pressure water with abrasive particles, like garnet, to enhance the cutting power and efficiency. Both types of machinery are widely used in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing, offering precise and efficient cutting solutions for a variety of materials.</p></p>
