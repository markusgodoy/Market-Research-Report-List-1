<p><p>The Signal Conditioner market comprises two main types: Retimers and Repeaters. Retimers are devices that regenerate and reshape digital signals, ensuring their integrity and enabling longer transmission distances. They correct any data loss or corruption due to signal deterioration. On the other hand, Repeaters amplify analog signals, compensating for any signal degradation or attenuation caused by transmission over long distances. Both Retimers and Repeaters play a crucial role in improving signal quality and extending the reach of signals in various industries, including telecommunications, networking, and data centers.</p></p>
