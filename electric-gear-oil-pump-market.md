<p><p>The Electric Gear Oil Pump market comprises two types: Integrated Pump and Separate Pump. An Integrated Pump refers to a system where the pump is integrated as a part of the gear housing, allowing oil to be circulated within the same unit. Conversely, a Separate Pump refers to a system where the pump is installed separately from the gear housing, with oil being circulated through external connections. Both types are commonly used in various industries for efficient oil circulation in machinery, but differ in their integration within the gear system.</p></p>
