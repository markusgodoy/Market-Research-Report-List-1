<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Semiconductor Temperature Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Voltage Output Temperature Sensors</li><li>Current Output Temperature Sensors</li><li>Digital Output Temperature Sensors</li><li>Resistance Output Silicon Temperature Sensors</li><li>Diode Temperature Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor temperature sensors market is divided into various types. Voltage output temperature sensors produce an output voltage proportional to the temperature. Current output temperature sensors generate an output current proportional to the temperature. Digital output temperature sensors provide a digital output signal representing the temperature. Resistance output silicon temperature sensors change their resistance with temperature. Diode temperature sensors utilize the junction voltage between two dissimilar materials to measure temperature. These different types cater to varying needs and applications in temperature sensing and control systems.</p></p>
