<p><p>A UAV (Unmanned Aerial Vehicle) platform refers to the combination of an unmanned aircraft system (UAS) and ground control station (GCS), which work together to enable various applications such as aerial photography, surveillance, surveying, inspection, and more. This integration enables autonomous flight, real-time decision-making capabilities, and enhanced data analysis.
