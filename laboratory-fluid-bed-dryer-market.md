The hot air helps in removing moisture from the materials, resulting in efficient and rapid drying.</p><p>The laboratory fluid bed dryer market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. Several factors are contributing to this growth. <p><p>The laboratory fluid bed dryer market can be categorized into single layer and multi-layer types. In the single layer market, the dryers have a single bed layer for drying materials. This type is suitable for processes that require a shorter drying time and is relatively simpler to operate. On the other hand, in the multi-layer market, the dryers have multiple bed layers, allowing for higher production capacity and increased efficiency. This type is ideal for processes that involve larger quantities of materials and longer drying times.</p></p>
