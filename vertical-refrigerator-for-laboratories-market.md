<p><p>The vertical refrigerator for laboratories market includes three main types: gas compression refrigerator, gas absorption refrigerator, and semiconductor refrigerator. Gas compression refrigerators work by compressing and expanding a gas to create a cooling effect. Gas absorption refrigerators use a combination of chemicals to achieve cooling, while semiconductor refrigerators utilize the principle of thermoelectric cooling. Each of these types is used in laboratory settings for various purposes, such as storing sensitive samples or maintaining controlled temperatures for experiments.</p></p>
