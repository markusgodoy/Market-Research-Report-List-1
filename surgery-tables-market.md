<p><p>Surgery tables are medical devices used in operating rooms to provide a stable and adjustable platform for surgical procedures. There are two main types of surgery tables: motorized and non-motorized. Motorized surgery tables are equipped with electric motors that allow easy and precise adjustments of the table height, tilt, and position. They offer greater flexibility and convenience for surgeons and medical staff during procedures. Non-motorized surgery tables, on the other hand, require manual adjustments and are generally more basic in design. Both types serve the purpose of facilitating safe and effective surgeries but differ in the level of automation and complexity.</p></p>
