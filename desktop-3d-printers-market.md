<p><strong>The Desktop 3D Printers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Material Extrusion Technology</li><li>Light Polymerization Technology</li><li>Other Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Desktop 3D printers are subcategorized into three main market types, namely Material Extrusion Technology, Light Polymerization Technology, and Other Technology. Material Extrusion Technology involves the melting and extrusion of filament-like materials, such as plastics, to build objects layer by layer. Light Polymerization Technology utilizes liquid resins that are hardened using ultraviolet light or laser, allowing for high precision and intricate details. The market for Other Technology includes various methods like powder-based, binder jetting, and sheet lamination, each employing different techniques for creating objects using different materials.</p></p>
