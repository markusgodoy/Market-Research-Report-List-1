<p><p>The current sense shunt resistors market is divided into two types: SMD (Surface Mount Device) type and Through Hole type. SMD type resistors are designed to be mounted directly onto the printed circuit board, providing a compact and space-saving solution. Through Hole type resistors, on the other hand, require insertion through holes in the circuit board and are generally larger in size. Both types serve the purpose of measuring current by producing a voltage drop proportionate to the current flowing through them, allowing for accurate current sensing in various applications.</p></p>
