<p><p>A double shear beam load cell is a type of load cell that is specifically designed to measure force or weight in applications where the load is applied to the load cell in both vertical and horizontal directions simultaneously. This type of load cell consists of two beams that are joined at the base and mounted perpendicular to the direction of force. The force applied to the load cell causes the beams to bend, which results in a change in resistance that is proportional to the applied force.</p><p>The double shear beam load cell market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
