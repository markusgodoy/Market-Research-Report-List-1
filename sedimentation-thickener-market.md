It is widely used in industries such as mining, wastewater treatment, and chemical processing. <p><p>Sedimentation thickener market types include column type and bridge type. Column type sedimentation thickeners are designed with vertical vessels, allowing for efficient separation of solids from liquids. They are commonly used in applications such as mineral processing and wastewater treatment. On the other hand, bridge type sedimentation thickeners consist of a supporting bridge that spans across the tank, with the central feedwell located under the bridge. This design enables effective settling of solids, making them suitable for various industries, including mining and chemical processing.</p></p>
