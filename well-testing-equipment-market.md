<p><ul><li>Downhole Equipment</li><li>Surface Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Well testing equipment is divided into two main markets: downhole equipment and surface equipment. Downhole equipment is used in the wellbore and includes tools such as downhole gauges, flow control devices, and packer systems. It helps in measuring and controlling fluid flow from the formation. Surface equipment, on the other hand, is used above ground and includes items like separators, flow meters, and storage tanks. It is responsible for separating and measuring the well fluids and storing them temporarily. Both types of equipment play a crucial role in evaluating the performance of oil and gas wells.</p></p>
