<p><p>The inhalation anesthetic market consists of various types such as Sevoflurane, Isoflurane, Desflurane, and others. Sevoflurane is commonly used during surgical procedures due to its rapid onset and minimal side effects. Isoflurane is preferred for its potency and cardiovascular stability. Desflurane offers a quick recovery due to its low solubility, making it suitable for outpatient procedures. The "others" category includes anesthetics like nitrous oxide and halothane.
