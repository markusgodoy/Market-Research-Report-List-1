<p><p>The Pediatric Radiology market consists of various types of imaging technologies used for diagnosing and treating medical conditions in children. Ultrasound utilizes sound waves to produce images of internal organs. X-Ray uses electromagnetic radiation to create images of bones and other dense tissues. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) uses magnetic fields and radio waves to generate detailed images of organs and tissues. Nuclear Medicine involves the use of radioactive substances that are injected or swallowed to produce images of internal structures. Computed Tomography (CT) provides cross-sectional images by combining X-Ray measurements from different angles.</p></p>
