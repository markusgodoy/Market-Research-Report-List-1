<p><p>Retractable awnings have two main market types: Manual retractable awnings and motorized retractable awnings. Manual retractable awnings are operated by hand, allowing users to extend or retract the awning manually. They are cost-effective and require less maintenance. On the other hand, motorized retractable awnings are operated using an electric motor. They provide the convenience of effortless operation, often controlled remotely or through a smart system. Although they are relatively more expensive, motorized retractable awnings offer enhanced functionality and ease of use.</p></p>
