<p><p>Screed plates are used in construction projects to level and smooth concrete surfaces. There are two main types of screed plates: hydraulic and mechanical. Hydraulic screed plates use a hydraulic system to adjust the plate's height and angle, providing flexibility and accuracy in leveling. Mechanical screed plates, on the other hand, rely on mechanical means such as manual adjustments or vibrations to achieve a flat finish. Both types are essential tools for achieving a precise and even concrete surface, but differ in their methods of operation.</p></p>
