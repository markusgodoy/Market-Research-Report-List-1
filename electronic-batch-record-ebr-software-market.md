<p><p>On-premise EBR software refers to a system where the software is installed and operated on the premises of the organization using it. This type of EBR provides full control and customization options to the user but requires a higher initial investment and ongoing maintenance costs. Hosted EBR, on the other hand, involves a third-party provider hosting the software on their servers and managing it for the organization.
