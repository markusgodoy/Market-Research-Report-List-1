<p><p>The Omega-6 Polyunsaturated Fatty Acids market is classified into two types: Linolenic Acid (LA) and Arachidonic Acid (AA). Linolenic Acid is an essential fatty acid found in vegetable oils and certain nuts and seeds. It plays a crucial role in the body's metabolism and is important for the structure and function of cell membranes. Arachidonic Acid, on the other hand, is derived from Linolenic Acid and is primarily found in animal products. It is involved in various physiological processes, including inflammation and immune response. Both LA and AA have significant market demand due to their various applications in industries such as food, pharmaceuticals, and cosmetics.</p></p>
