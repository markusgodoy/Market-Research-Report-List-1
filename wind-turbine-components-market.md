<p><p>The wind turbine components market consists of various types of components. The generator is a crucial component that converts the wind's kinetic energy into electrical energy. The nacelle houses the generator, gearbox, and other key components, and is responsible for controlling the wind turbine's movements. The tower provides support to the nacelle and rotor blades, enabling the turbine to capture the maximum wind energy. The rotor blades, which spin when the wind blows, harness the wind's energy and convert it into rotational motion. Other components may include control systems, yaw mechanism, and braking systems.</p></p>
