<p><p>The Preterm Birth Diagnostic Test Kits market includes various types of kits used to diagnose preterm birth. The fFN (Fetal Fibronectin) test kits measure the presence of a protein in vaginal discharge, indicating the risk of preterm birth. PAMG-1 (Placental Alpha Microglobulin-1) test kits detect a specific protein in vaginal fluid to identify women at risk of premature delivery. IGFBP 1 (Insulin-like Growth Factor Binding Protein-1) test kits measure a protein in cervicovaginal fluid to predict preterm birth. The "Others" category includes additional diagnostic test kits used for assessing the risk of preterm birth.</p></p>
