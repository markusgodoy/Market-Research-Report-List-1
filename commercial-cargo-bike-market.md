<p><p>A commercial cargo bike refers to a type of bicycle designed specifically for transporting goods or cargo. The market growth can be attributed to several factors. Firstly, there is an increasing emphasis on sustainable transportation solutions, driven by environmental concerns and efforts to reduce traffic congestion in urban areas. Commercial cargo bikes offer an eco-friendly and efficient alternative to motorized vehicles for short-distance deliveries.</p><p>Furthermore, advancements in technology have contributed to the market growth. Electric cargo bikes have gained popularity as they provide a convenient way to transport goods even over longer distances or hilly terrains. The electric-assist feature eliminates the need for physical exertion, making it easier for riders to handle heavier loads.</p><p>Another trend in the commercial cargo bike market is the rising adoption of last-mile delivery services.
