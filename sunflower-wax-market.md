<p><p>Sunflower wax, also known as Helianthus Annuus (Sunflower) Seed Wax, is a natural wax derived from sunflower seeds. Sunflower wax finds applications in various sectors such as cosmetics, food, pharmaceuticals, and candles. In the cosmetics industry, it is used as an emollient, viscosity regulator, and moisturizing agent in various formulations, including lip balms, lotions, creams, and ointments. The rising trend of using natural ingredients in cosmetic products is expected to fuel the demand for sunflower wax.</p><p>In the food industry, sunflower wax is utilized as a food additive and coating agent.
