<p><strong>The Radiant Floor Heating System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Installation above Wooden Floor</li><li>Installation Under Subfloor</li><li>Ordinary Concrete Slab or Underground Installation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The market for radiant floor heating systems includes three main installation types. Installation above a wooden floor involves placing heating panels or mats on top of the existing floor, providing efficient heat distribution. Installation under a subfloor involves placing the heating system beneath the floor, typically in new constructions or during renovations. Ordinary concrete slab or underground installation requires embedding the heating system within the concrete slab or the ground itself. All these options offer effective and customizable heating solutions for different types of flooring and construction preferences.</p></p>
