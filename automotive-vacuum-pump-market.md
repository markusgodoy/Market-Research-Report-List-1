<p><ul><li>Electric Vacuum Pumps</li><li>Mechanical Vacuum Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive vacuum pump market consists of two main types: electric vacuum pumps and mechanical vacuum pumps. Electric vacuum pumps are driven by an electric motor and provide vacuum for various automotive systems, such as brake boosters and HVAC systems. They are more efficient and quieter compared to mechanical vacuum pumps. Mechanical vacuum pumps, on the other hand, are driven by the engine's mechanical power and produce vacuum through mechanical means, such as the rotation of a rotor or piston. They are often used in older vehicles or heavy-duty applications.</p></p>
