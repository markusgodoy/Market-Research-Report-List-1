<p><p>The cleanroom finger cots market consists of two main types - nitrile finger cots and latex finger cots. Nitrile finger cots are made of synthetic rubber, providing excellent chemical resistance and protection against oils, acids, and other substances. They are suitable for use in cleanrooms and other sensitive environments. Latex finger cots, on the other hand, are made from natural rubber and offer good flexibility and tactile sensitivity. They are commonly used in cleanrooms for general protection and ease of use. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining hygiene and preventing contamination in cleanroom environments.</p></p>
