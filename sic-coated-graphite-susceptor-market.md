<p><strong>The SiC Coated Graphite Susceptor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pancake Susceptor</li><li>Barrel Susceptor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SiC coated graphite susceptor market includes two main types: pancake susceptor and barrel susceptor. A pancake susceptor is a flat round-shaped substrate made of graphite coated with silicon carbide (SiC), used in the semiconductor industry for chemical vapor deposition (CVD) processes. It facilitates uniform heating of silicon wafers during the manufacturing of semiconductor devices. On the other hand, a barrel susceptor is a cylindrical-shaped substrate with a SiC-coated graphite inner surface, typically used in the epitaxy process to grow high-quality crystalline layers of semiconductors. Both types play a crucial role in the production of semiconductor devices.</p></p>
