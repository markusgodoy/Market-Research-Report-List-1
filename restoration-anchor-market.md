<p><p>Restoration anchor market types include mechanical restraining anchors, mechanical repair anchors, and helical tie anchors. Mechanical restraining anchors are designed to provide structural support and stability for damaged or weakened structures. Mechanical repair anchors are utilized to fix structural defects or cracks in a building or infrastructure. Helical tie anchors use screw-like helical plates to provide lateral load resistance and stabilize or reinforce structures. Each of these anchor types plays a crucial role in restoration projects and ensures the safety and longevity of various structures.</p></p>
