<p><p>The Liquid Chromatograph Market consists of three main types: High-Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC), Ultra High-Performance Liquid Chromatography (UHPLC), and Medium-Performance Liquid Chromatography (MPLC). HPLC is a widely used technique for separating, identifying, and quantifying components in a mixture. UHPLC further enhances the capabilities of HPLC by providing higher resolution and faster analysis times. MPLC, on the other hand, is a cost-effective alternative with lower efficiency compared to HPLC. Each type serves different needs and requirements in various industries and laboratories.</p></p>
