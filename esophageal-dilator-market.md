<p><p>The esophageal dilator market consists of two main types: mechanical esophageal dilators and balloon esophageal dilators. Mechanical esophageal dilators are instruments used to widen the esophagus mechanically, typically by using a set of rigid rods or bougies. On the other hand, balloon esophageal dilators use inflatable balloons to expand the esophagus gradually. Both types are employed in medical procedures to treat various esophageal conditions, such as strictures or narrowing of the esophagus. These dilators provide different options for physicians to choose from depending on the patient's specific needs.</p></p>
