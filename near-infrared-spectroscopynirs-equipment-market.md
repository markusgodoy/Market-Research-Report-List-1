<p><p>Near-infrared spectroscopy (NIRS) equipment market consists of various types including FT-NIR spectroscopy, dispersive NIR spectroscopy, and other technologies such as acousto-optic tunable filters (AOTF) and filter-based systems. FT-NIR spectroscopy uses Fourier transform technology for high-resolution analysis, while dispersive NIR spectroscopy relies on diffraction grating. Other technologies like AOTF and filter systems offer alternative methods for spectral analysis. Each type of NIRS equipment has unique advantages and applications, catering to different needs in industries like pharmaceuticals, agriculture, and food safety.</p></p>
