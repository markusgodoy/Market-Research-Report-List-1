<p><p>Cashmere knitting yarn market is divided into two types: carded yarns and combed yarns. Carded yarns are made by passing the fiber through carding machines to align them in a parallel manner, resulting in a less uniform texture. This type of yarn is often used for rustic and textured garments. On the other hand, combed yarns involve the removal of shorter fibers and impurities, resulting in a smoother and finer yarn. Combed yarns are preferred for luxurious and high-quality products, such as fine cashmere garments.</p></p>
