<p><p>Fistula needles are medical devices utilized for the creation of vascular access for hemodialysis treatment. The needles are specifically designed to be inserted into the arteriovenous fistula, providing optimal performance during the dialysis process.</p><p>The Fistula Needles Market is projected to exhibit significant growth over the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to several factors such as the rising prevalence of end-stage renal disease (ESRD) and chronic kidney diseases (CKD), increasing geriatric population, and advancements in healthcare infrastructure. One prominent trend is the increasing preference for buttonhole cannulation technique, which involves creating a permanent hole in the skin over the fistula to insert needles.
