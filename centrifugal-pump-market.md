<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Centrifugal pump market types include axial flow pumps, mixed or radial flow pumps, peripheral pumps, and jet pumps. Axial flow pumps are designed to move fluids in a parallel direction to the pump shaft. Mixed or radial flow pumps move fluids both radially and axially. Peripheral pumps are used for low-pressure applications and have a peripheral impeller. Jet pumps are used for deep well applications and utilize high-velocity jets to create suction and draw fluids. These different types cater to various pumping requirements in sectors such as water treatment, agriculture, and oil and gas.</p></p>
