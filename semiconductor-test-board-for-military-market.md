<p><strong>The Semiconductor Test Board for Military Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Probe Card</li><li>Load Board</li><li>Burn-inBoard(BIB)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the military market, there are three types of semiconductor test boards: probe cards, load boards, and burn-in boards (BIB). Probe cards are used to test the electrical performance of semiconductor devices and facilitate contact between the device and the testing equipment. Load boards provide electrical connections and simulate real-world operating conditions for testing the performance of a semiconductor device. Burn-in boards (BIB) are designed to subject the semiconductor devices to extreme conditions for an extended period to ensure their reliability and performance under harsh environments.</p></p>
