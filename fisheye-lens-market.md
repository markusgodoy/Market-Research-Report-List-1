<p><p>The fisheye lens market consists of two main types: circular fisheye lens and diagonal fisheye lens. Circular fisheye lenses are characterized by a circular image within the frame, providing a 180-degree fisheye effect. They are commonly used for creative and artistic purposes. On the other hand, diagonal fisheye lenses capture a wider angle of view in a rectangular frame, resulting in a distorted image. They are often preferred by photographers who require a broader field of view, such as in architectural or real estate photography.</p></p>
