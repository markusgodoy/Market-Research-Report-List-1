<p><p>Wrapping and packaging machines are used in various industries to automate the process of enclosing products for protection and shipment. The market can be divided into horizontal and vertical types. Horizontal machines wrap products by sealing them horizontally, suitable for items like candy bars or biscuits. In contrast, vertical machines seal products in a standing position, typically used for items like bags of potato chips or bottles. The choice between horizontal or vertical machines depends on the shape, size, and orientation of the products being packaged.</p></p>
