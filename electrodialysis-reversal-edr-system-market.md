<p><p>The Electrodialysis Reversal (EDR) System Market comprises two types of systems: Continuous Electrodialysis and Batch Electrodialysis. Continuous Electrodialysis systems operate continuously, removing salts and other dissolved substances from a solution using a series of ion-exchange membranes. This type of system is suitable for large-scale industrial applications. On the other hand, Batch Electrodialysis systems operate in a batch-wise manner, making it suitable for smaller-scale applications or situations where intermittent treatment is required. Both systems are utilized in various industries for desalination, wastewater treatment, and the production of pure water.</p></p>
