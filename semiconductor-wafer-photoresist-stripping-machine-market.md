<p><p>The Semiconductor Wafer Photoresist Stripping Machine market comprises two types: Dry Process Machine and Wet Process Machine. Dry Process machines use chemical vapor deposition to remove the photoresist, making it suitable for wafers with delicate structures. It ensures minimal damage and high precision. On the other hand, Wet Process machines use liquid chemicals, making them ideal for large-scale production due to their high throughput capacity. They can strip photoresist using immersion or spray methods, providing a cost-effective solution for semiconductor manufacturers.</p></p>
