<p><p>The dehumidity unit market is divided into several types, including cooling dehumidifiers, rotary dehumidifiers, pipeline dehumidifiers, and electroosmosis dehumidifiers. Cooling dehumidifiers utilize refrigeration technology to cool the air below the dew point, causing moisture to condense and be collected. Rotary dehumidifiers use rotating desiccant wheels to absorb moisture from the air. Pipeline dehumidifiers are used in large-scale industrial applications and work by drying the air using a network of pipelines. Electroosmosis dehumidifiers use electroosmotic technology to remove moisture from the air. These different types cater to specific cooling and drying needs in various industries.</p></p>
