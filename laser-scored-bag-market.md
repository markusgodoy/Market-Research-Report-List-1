<p><strong>The Laser Scored Bag Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inline Laser Scoring</li><li>Transverse Laser Scoring</li><li>Freeform Laser Scoring</li><li>Micro-perforation Scoring</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laser scored bag market can be categorized into different types. Inline laser scoring involves creating scores or perforations in a bag in a straight line during the manufacturing process. Transverse laser scoring refers to the creation of scores or perforations across the bag. Freeform laser scoring involves the creation of custom-designed scores or perforations on the bag. Micro-perforation scoring involves creating tiny holes in the bag to enhance its functionality. Lastly, the "Others" market includes any other types of laser scoring techniques not mentioned above.</p></p>
