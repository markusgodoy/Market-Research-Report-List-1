<p><p>The Benchtop Parts Washer market can be categorized into two main types: solvent-based and water/aqueous-based. Solvent-based parts washers use chemicals or solvents to clean and degrease parts. These machines are highly effective at removing tough contaminants but may have environmental and health concerns. On the other hand, water/aqueous-based parts washers use water-based detergents or cleaning agents to clean parts. These machines are considered to be more environmentally friendly and safer to use but may not be as effective on certain types of contaminants compared to solvent-based washers.</p></p>
