<p><strong>The Run Flat Tires Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-Supporting</li><li>Support Ring System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The run-flat tires market includes two main types: self-supporting and support ring system. Self-supporting tires are designed with reinforced sidewalls that can bear the vehicle's weight even when the tire loses air pressure. This enables the driver to continue driving for a limited distance to reach a nearby service station for tire replacement or repair. On the other hand, support ring system tires feature an added support ring that is attached to the inner circumference of the wheel, allowing the tire to maintain its shape and functionality in the event of a puncture.</p></p>
