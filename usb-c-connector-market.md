<p><p>The market for USB-C connectors can be classified into two main types: Type C male connectors and Type C female connectors. The Type C male connector is designed to be inserted into the USB-C port of a device, enabling it to connect to other devices or chargers. On the other hand, the Type C female connector is designed to receive the male connector, allowing devices or chargers to be connected to it. These connectors have gained popularity due to their versatility, fast data transfer rates, and ability to charge devices.</p></p>
