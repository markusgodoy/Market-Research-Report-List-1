<p><p>The Critical Care Equipment (CCE) market comprises various types of medical devices used to monitor and support critically ill patients. Patient monitors track vital signs such as heart rate and blood pressure. Medical ventilators assist in breathing for patients unable to do so on their own. Dialysis machines remove waste and excess fluid from the blood in patients with kidney failure. IV pumps deliver fluids and medication to patients intravenously. Infusion pumps administer controlled amounts of medication. Suction apparatuses are employed to remove fluids or secretions from the airways. Other CCE includes devices like defibrillators and anesthesia machines.</p></p>
