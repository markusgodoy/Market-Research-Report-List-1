<p><p>Ditcher market types include wheel ditcher and chain ditcher. A wheel ditcher is a machine with large wheels that digs trenches and is commonly used for agricultural drainage purposes. It is efficient in loose or sandy soil. On the other hand, a chain ditcher uses a rotating chain with cutting teeth to create channels in denser or compacted soil. It is often utilized in construction projects and is capable of cutting through harder surfaces. Both types cater to specific soil conditions and serve the purpose of creating ditches or trenches for various applications.</p></p>
