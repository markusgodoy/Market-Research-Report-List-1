Automated External Defibrillator (AED) is used to restore heart rhythm during sudden cardiac arrests. Extracorporeal Oxygenators support oxygenation and remove carbon dioxide from the blood in cases of severe lung dysfunction. Ventilators assist breathing in patients unable to do so naturally. The "Others" category includes diverse devices like dialysis machines, cardiac pumps, and hyperbaric chambers. These systems play a critical role in emergency medical care, supporting vital bodily functions to save lives.</p></p>
