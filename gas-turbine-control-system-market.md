<p><p>The Gas Turbine Control System Market consists of two main types: Single Axis System and Dual Axis System. In a Single Axis System, the control is focused on the turbine shaft's rotational speed, ensuring it runs at a stable level. On the other hand, a Dual Axis System monitors both the rotational speed and the exhaust gas temperature, enabling precise control to maximize efficiency and reduce emissions. Both systems play a crucial role in enhancing the performance and reliability of gas turbines in various industrial applications.</p></p>
