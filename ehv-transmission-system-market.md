<p><p>The EHV transmission system market includes three main applications: overhead transmission, underground transmission, and subsea transmission. Underground transmission involves the placement of cables beneath the ground, offering advantages like reduced visual impact and protection against weather conditions. Subsea transmission entails the installation of cables under the ocean for transmitting electricity between offshore wind farms or connecting countries separated by water bodies, aiding in clean energy transfer and interconnection.</p></p>
