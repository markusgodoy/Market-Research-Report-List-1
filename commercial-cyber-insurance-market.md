<p><strong>The Commercial Cyber Insurance Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stand-alone Cyber Insurance</li><li>Packaged Cyber Insurance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial cyber insurance market includes two main types: stand-alone cyber insurance and packaged cyber insurance. Stand-alone cyber insurance provides coverage for losses and damages related to cyber risks only. It is a specialized policy that focuses solely on cyber threats and provides tailored coverage based on the unique needs of the business. On the other hand, packaged cyber insurance is bundled together with other existing insurance policies, such as general liability or property insurance. It offers a more comprehensive coverage approach by combining cyber risk coverage with other traditional policy coverages.</p></p>
