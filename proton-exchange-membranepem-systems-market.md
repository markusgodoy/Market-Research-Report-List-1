<p><p>Proton Exchange Membrane (PEM) Systems market consists of two types of membranes, namely Polyaromatic Polymers Membrane and Partially Fluorinated Polymers Membrane. Polyaromatic Polymers Membrane is a type of PEM that exhibits high proton conductivity due to its ability to retain water. It is cost-effective and commonly used in fuel cells. On the other hand, Partially Fluorinated Polymers Membrane is known for its exceptional chemical resistance and durability, making it suitable for harsh operating conditions. It is often utilized in applications requiring high temperature and pressure tolerance.</p></p>
