<p><p>The Radial Piston Displacement Pump market consists of two main types: Fixed Displacement and Variable Displacement. Fixed Displacement pumps have a constant output regardless of the system's pressure, making them suitable for applications with stable flow requirements. In contrast, Variable Displacement pumps allow adjustment of the output to meet changing flow demands. This flexibility makes Variable Displacement pumps suitable for systems that experience varying flow rates or require control over the fluid flow. Both types offer distinct advantages depending on the specific needs of the application.</p></p>
