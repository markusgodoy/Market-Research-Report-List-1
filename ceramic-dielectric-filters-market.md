<p><p>Ceramic Dielectric Filters are devices used to manipulate the flow of electrical signals in electronic systems. Bandstop Filters are a type of Ceramic Dielectric Filter that block specific frequency bands while allowing others to pass through. These filters are commonly used to eliminate interference or unwanted signals in communication systems. On the other hand, Band-pass Filters are also a type of Ceramic Dielectric Filter that allow a specific range of frequencies to pass through while rejecting others. They are used in applications such as wireless communication and audio systems.</p></p>
