<p><p>The bioactive coating devices market is categorized into different types based on their functionalities. Anti-microbial coatings are designed to prevent the growth of microorganisms on the device's surface, reducing the risk of infections. Hydrophilic coatings enhance the device's compatibility with bodily fluids, improving its lubricity and reducing friction. Drug eluting coatings release medication to the surrounding tissues, promoting healing or preventing complications. Other coatings, like anti-thrombogenic coatings, help prevent blood clotting. These coatings play a crucial role in enhancing the performance and safety of medical devices.</p></p>
