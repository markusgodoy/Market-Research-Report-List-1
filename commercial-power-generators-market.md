<p><p>The commercial power generators market consists of two primary types: portable generators and standby generators. Portable generators are designed to be easily transported and are commonly used in scenarios such as camping trips or construction sites where temporary power is required. On the other hand, standby generators are installed permanently and automatically provide backup power during outages or when the main power source fails. Standby generators are commonly used in commercial buildings, hospitals, or data centers where uninterrupted power supply is crucial.</p></p>
