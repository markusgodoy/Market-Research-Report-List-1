<p><ul><li>NIR Camera</li><li>SWIR Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The micro cameras market includes different types of cameras, such as NIR (Near Infrared) cameras and SWIR (Short-Wave Infrared) cameras. NIR cameras are designed to capture images in the near-infrared spectrum, which is invisible to the human eye but useful for various applications like biological imaging, food inspection, and surveillance. On the other hand, SWIR cameras operate in the short-wave infrared spectrum, allowing them to identify objects and materials based on their unique reflection properties. SWIR cameras find applications in areas like industrial inspection, semiconductor inspection, and defense.</p></p>
