<p><p>A vaccine vial is a small glass container used to store and transport vaccines and other medicines. It is usually made of borosilicate glass which is resistant to thermal shock and therefore suitable for sterilization and long-term storage. The vial is sealed with a rubber stopper and aluminum cap to maintain sterility and prevent leakage.</p><p>The vaccine vial market has been experiencing significant growth due to the increasing demand for vaccines worldwide. Vaccines are crucial in the prevention and control of various infectious diseases, and as the global population continues to grow, the demand for vaccines is expected to rise. This, in turn, drives the demand for vaccine vials.</p><p>Furthermore, the rise in government initiatives aimed at immunization programs in developing countries is also contributing to the growth of the vaccine vial market. These initiatives aim to provide vaccines to a larger population, including remote and underprivileged areas, thereby increasing the need for vaccine vials.</p><p>In terms of market trends, there is a growing preference for prefilled vaccine vials due to their convenience and ease of use.
