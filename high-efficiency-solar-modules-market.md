<p><strong>The High-efficiency Solar Modules Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HJT Cells</li><li>IBC Cells</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-efficiency solar modules market is divided into two types: HJT cells and IBC cells. HJT cells, or heterojunction cells, are made by combining different materials with varying bandgaps, allowing for a greater absorption of light and higher efficiency. They have low temperature coefficient and excellent low-light performance. IBC cells, or interdigitated back contact cells, have a unique design where all contacts are on the backside, reducing shading and maximizing efficiency. They offer high power output and good temperature coefficient, making them ideal for space-constrained installations.</p></p>
