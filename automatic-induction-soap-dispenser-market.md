<p><p>The automatic induction soap dispenser market consists of two main types: desktop and floor-standing. Desktop dispensers are compact and designed to be placed on surfaces like countertops or bathroom sinks. They are suitable for personal use and small spaces. On the other hand, floor-standing dispensers are taller and sturdier, often used in public places like hospitals, malls, or office buildings. These dispensers have a larger capacity to accommodate high foot traffic and are commonly found in restrooms or entrances. Both types use induction technology to detect hand motion and dispense soap without physical contact.</p></p>
