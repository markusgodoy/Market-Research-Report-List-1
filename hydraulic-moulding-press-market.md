<p><p>Hydraulic moulding press refers to a machine used in various industries for molding processes. There are two main types of hydraulic moulding presses - up stroke and down stroke. Up stroke moulding press applies pressure from the top, forcing the material into the mold below. In contrast, down stroke moulding press applies pressure from the bottom, pushing the material towards the mold above. These presses offer different advantages depending on the specific molding requirements, such as shape complexity, material characteristics, or production speed.</p></p>
