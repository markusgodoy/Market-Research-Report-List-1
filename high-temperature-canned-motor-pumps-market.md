<p><p>The high temperature canned motor pumps market consists of three main types: water cooled pumps, air cooled pumps, and non-cooled pumps. Water cooled pumps utilize water as a cooling medium to prevent overheating of the motor. Air cooled pumps use air as the cooling medium, which is circulated around the motor to dissipate heat. Non-cooled pumps do not incorporate any cooling mechanism and are reliant on the surrounding environment for heat dissipation. These different types offer diverse cooling options for high temperature applications depending on the specific requirements and conditions.</p></p>
