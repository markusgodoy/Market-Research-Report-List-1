<p><p>Hospitality POS terminals are divided into two main types - mobile and wireless POS terminals, and fixed POS terminals. Mobile and wireless POS terminals are portable devices that allow servers to take orders and process payments from anywhere in the restaurant. This type of terminal is ideal for businesses that need flexibility and mobility. Fixed POS terminals, on the other hand, are stationary devices typically found at the front counter or bar. They are best suited for businesses with a high volume of transactions and limited space for mobility.</p></p>
