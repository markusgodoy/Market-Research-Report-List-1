<p><ul><li>Braided</li><li>Monofilament</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fishing lines market consists primarily of two main types: braided and monofilament lines. Braided lines are known for their strength and durability, as they are made by weaving together multiple strands of material like nylon or Dyneema. On the other hand, monofilament lines are single strand lines that are more stretchy and have more buoyancy. Each type caters to different fishing styles and preferences, with braided lines often preferred for heavy-duty fishing and monofilament lines for more finesse and sensitivity.</p></p>
