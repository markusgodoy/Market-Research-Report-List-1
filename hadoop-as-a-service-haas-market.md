<p><p>Hadoop-as-a-Service (HaaS) refers to cloud-based platforms that offer Hadoop infrastructure and services. This option is popular among organizations with existing Hadoop expertise but requires additional time and maintenance efforts. On the other hand, the Pure Play (PP) market type involves specialized HaaS providers taking care of the complete implementation and management of Hadoop clusters, allowing users to focus solely on utilizing the data and insights generated.</p></p>
