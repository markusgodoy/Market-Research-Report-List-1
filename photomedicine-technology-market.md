<p><p>Photomedicine technology encompasses various types of devices and techniques used in medical treatments involving light. Laser technology utilizes intense beams of monochromatic light to cut, vaporize, or seal tissue, aiding in surgeries or therapies. Dichroic light involves the use of specially coated filters that selectively reflect or transmit light of specific wavelengths for targeted treatment of skin conditions or phototherapy. Light-emitting diodes (LEDs) emit specific colors of light to stimulate cellular activity or deliver targeted therapeutic benefits like wound healing or pain relief. These technologies contribute to the advancement of non-invasive and effective medical procedures.</p></p>
