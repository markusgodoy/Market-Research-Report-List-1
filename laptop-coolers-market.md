<p><p>The laptop coolers market can be categorized into three types: active laptop coolers, passive laptop coolers, and multi-purpose laptop coolers. Active laptop coolers use fans to circulate air and cool down the laptop, providing active cooling. Passive laptop coolers, on the other hand, are designed to elevate the laptop and improve airflow, using natural convection to passively cool it. Multi-purpose laptop coolers combine both active and passive cooling methods, offering a versatile cooling solution for various laptop models and user preferences.</p></p>
