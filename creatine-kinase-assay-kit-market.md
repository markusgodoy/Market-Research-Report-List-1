<p><p>Creatine Kinase Assay Kit is a type of diagnostic tool used to measure the levels of creatine kinase in biological samples. The market for these kits can be categorized into two types: Enzyme Coupling Method and Colorimetry. The Enzyme Coupling Method involves linking the enzymatic reaction of creatine kinase to another enzyme, resulting in the production of a measurable product. On the other hand, Colorimetry utilizes a color change reaction to detect the presence of creatine kinase. Both methods provide accurate and efficient ways to analyze creatine kinase levels in various applications.</p></p>
