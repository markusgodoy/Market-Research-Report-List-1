<p><p>The drilling tools market includes various types of tools used in the drilling process such as drill bits, drill collars, drilling jars, drilling motors, drilling tubulars, drill swivels, drill reamers and stabilizers, mechanical thrusters, and shock tools. Drill bits are used to cut through rocks and sediments, while drill collars provide weight to the drill string. Drilling jars reduce sticking of the drill pipe, while drilling motors help rotate the bit. Drill tubulars connect various components of the drill string, while drill swivels allow the rotation of the drill string. Drill reamers and stabilizers ensure the smoothness of the wellbore. Mechanical thrusters are used for enhanced drilling performance, and shock tools absorb shocks and vibrations during drilling operations.</p></p>
