<p><p>A bone densitometer is a medical device used to measure bone density and diagnose osteoporosis, a condition characterized by low bone mass and increased risk of fractures. It works by emitting low-energy x-rays to assess the mineral content and strength of bones. The results obtained from a bone densitometer help healthcare professionals evaluate bone health, prognosis, and determine the most appropriate course of treatment for patients.</p><p>The global bone densitometer market is projected to grow at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 8.5% during the forecast period. In addition, technological advancements in imaging techniques, such as the introduction of dual-energy x-ray absorptiometry (DXA) systems, are further propelling market growth.</p><p>Moreover, increasing awareness about preventive healthcare and the adoption of healthy lifestyles are contributing to the market expansion.
