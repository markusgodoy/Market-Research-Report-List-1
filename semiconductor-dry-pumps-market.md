<p><p>Semiconductor dry pumps are used in the semiconductor manufacturing industry to remove gas and particles from vacuum chambers. There are different types of pumps available in the market. The cam type pump relies on the movement of vanes to create a vacuum. The claw type pump uses two claw-shaped rotors to compress gas. The combined type pump combines the features of Roots and claw pumps for higher performance. The screw pump utilizes two helical screws to create a vacuum. These different pump types offer varying levels of efficiency and capabilities in removing gas and particles.</p></p>
