<p><p>The market for trowels can be categorized into two main types: walk-behind trowels and ride-on trowels. Walk-behind trowels are handheld machines used for finishing concrete surfaces, such as floors and pavements, by smoothing and leveling them. These trowels are usually operated by a person walking behind the machine. On the other hand, ride-on trowels are larger and have a seat for the operator to ride on. They are used for larger concrete surfaces and provide more power, efficiency, and control.</p></p>
