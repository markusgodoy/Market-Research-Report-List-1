SPECT is a nuclear imaging technique that involves injecting a radioactive tracer into the patient's body, which emits gamma rays. These probes help in capturing the gamma rays emitted by the tracer and creating detailed three-dimensional images of internal organs.</p><p>The global Single Photon Emission Computerized Tomography (SPECT) probes market is expected to experience substantial growth during the forecast period. The market growth can be attributed to several factors, including technological advancements in imaging modalities, increasing prevalence of chronic diseases, and a growing geriatric population. Manufacturers are focusing on integrating SPECT with other imaging technologies like computed tomography (CT) and magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) to provide comprehensive and accurate diagnosis. These hybrid systems offer enhanced visualization capabilities, increased diagnostic accuracy, and improved patient outcomes.</p><p>Additionally, there is a rising demand for personalized medicine, leading to the adoption of molecular imaging techniques like SPECT.
