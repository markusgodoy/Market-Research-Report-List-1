<p><p>Lighting Source Market Types refers to the various lighting technologies available in the market. LED (Light Emitting Diode) lighting offers energy efficiency, long lifespan, and flexibility in design, making it widely popular. HID (High-Intensity Discharge) lamps produce light through an electric arc, providing high brightness and color rendition. HPS (High-Pressure Sodium) lamps are commonly used for outdoor lighting due to their high efficiency and long life. Induction lamps use electromagnetic fields to excite mercury atoms, releasing UV light, which is converted to visible light using phosphor coatings.</p></p>
