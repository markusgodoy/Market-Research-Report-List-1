<p><p>VRLA (Valve Regulated Lead Acid) batteries are a type of rechargeable battery used in a variety of applications. There are two main types of VRLA batteries: Absorbed Glass Mat (AGM) batteries and Gel batteries. AGM batteries have fiberglass mats that absorb and hold the battery electrolyte, while Gel batteries have a silica gel that suspends the electrolyte. AGM batteries are known for their high-performance, low maintenance, and durability, making them suitable for various uses. Gel batteries, on the other hand, offer superior deep-cycle performance and are commonly used in solar systems and other applications that require continuous power.</p></p>
