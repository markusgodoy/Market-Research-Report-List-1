Roots pumps are widely used for high vacuum processes, such as maintaining a hydrogen atmosphere in fuel cells. Claw pumps are known for their oil-free operation and are suitable for applications that require a clean and dry pumping environment. Scroll pumps provide high efficiency and low noise operation, making them ideal for hydrogen circulation systems.
