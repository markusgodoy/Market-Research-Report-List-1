<p><p>Watertube boilers are a type of boiler that utilize water-filled tubes to create heat and generate steam. <p><p>Watertube boilers are classified based on their mounting type, with vertical and horizontal being the two main types. Vertical boilers have the tube assembly vertically arranged, while horizontal boilers have a horizontal tube arrangement. Watertube boilers are also categorized based on their shape type, such as O-Type, D-Type, and A-Type. O-Type boilers have a single large drum and a symmetrical tube arrangement. D-Type boilers have two drums and a bent tube arrangement. A-Type boilers have a small drum and a large furnace volume for increased thermal efficiency.</p></p>
