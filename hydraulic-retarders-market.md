<p><p>The hydraulic retarders market encompasses three main types of retarders: single-stage, dual-stage, and multi-stage. Single-stage retarders offer a simple design and operate by using oil to generate friction and slow down the vehicle. Dual-stage retarders have two chambers and allow for increased braking power at higher speeds. Lastly, multi-stage retarders have multiple chambers and enable precise control and enhanced braking efficiency. These different types cater to the varying needs and requirements of vehicles in terms of braking power and control.</p></p>
