<p><p>Order fulfillment software market can be categorized into three types: web-based, cloud-based, and on-premise. Web-based software is accessible through a web browser and requires an internet connection to operate. Cloud-based software, on the other hand, is stored and accessed remotely through the internet, providing greater flexibility and scalability. On-premise software is installed and operated on the company's own servers, allowing for direct control and customization but requiring higher upfront investment and maintenance costs. These different types offer businesses various options to choose from based on their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
