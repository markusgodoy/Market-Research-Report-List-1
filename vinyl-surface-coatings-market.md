<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Vinyl Surface Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-based</li><li>Waterborne (Latex)</li><li>Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vinyl surface coatings can be categorized into three main types based on the solvent used: solvent-based, waterborne (latex), and powder coatings. Solvent-based coatings use organic solvents as a carrier for the coating material. Waterborne coatings use water as a carrier, making them more environmentally friendly than solvent-based coatings. Powder coatings are applied as a dry powder and cured through heat, making them durable and resistant to chipping and scratching. Each type of coating offers unique advantages and is suitable for different applications.</p></p>
