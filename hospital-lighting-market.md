<p><p>Hospital Lighting market types include troffers, surface-mounted lights, and surgical lamps. Troffers are recessed lighting fixtures that provide uniform lighting in hospital hallways and patient rooms. Surface-mounted lights are fixtures that are mounted directly on the ceiling, offering bright and efficient lighting in areas such as treatment rooms and waiting areas. Surgical lamps are specialized lights used in operating rooms, providing bright and focused lighting for surgical procedures. These different types of hospital lighting options are designed to meet specific lighting needs in different areas of the hospital facility.</p></p>
