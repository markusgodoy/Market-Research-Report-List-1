<p><strong>The Secure Sockets Layer(SSL) Certification Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>OV SSL Certificate</li><li>DV SSL Certificate</li><li>EV SSL Certificate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>SSL certification market can be divided into three main types- OV (Organization Validated) SSL Certificate, DV (Domain Validated) SSL Certificate, and EV (Extended Validation) SSL Certificate. OV SSL Certificates require verification of the organization owning the domain. DV SSL Certificates only verify the domain ownership. EV SSL Certificates provide the highest level of authentication by verifying both domain ownership and organization information. Each type caters to different levels of security needs and trust for online businesses and websites.</p></p>
