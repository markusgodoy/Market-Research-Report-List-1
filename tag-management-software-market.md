<p><p>Tag management software market can be categorized into two types: on-premises and cloud-based. On-premises tag management software operates within a company's internal infrastructure, requiring dedicated servers and IT maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based tag management software is hosted on remote servers and accessed over the internet, eliminating the need for companies to manage the software in-house. Both types offer benefits; on-premises provides more control and security, while cloud-based offers scalability, accessibility, and reduced IT costs.
