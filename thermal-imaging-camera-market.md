<p><strong>The Thermal Imaging Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Uncooled Thermal Imaging Camera</li><li>Cooled Thermal Imaging Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal imaging cameras are divided into two main types: uncooled thermal imaging cameras and cooled thermal imaging cameras. </p><p>Uncooled thermal imaging cameras use microbolometer technology, where the sensor detects heat radiation emitted by objects. These cameras do not require cooling, making them smaller, lighter, and more affordable. They are commonly used for applications like home inspections, electrical troubleshooting, and firefighting.</p><p>Cooled thermal imaging cameras, on the other hand, use a cooled detector to improve sensitivity and image quality. These cameras are more expensive and are used in high-end applications such as military surveillance, research, and industrial inspections. Their cooling system allows for higher resolution and longer detection ranges.</p></p>
