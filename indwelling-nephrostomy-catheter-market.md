<p><p>An indwelling nephrostomy catheter is a tube that is inserted into the kidney through a small incision in the back. The catheter is secured in place by a balloon, which is inflated inside the bladder.</p><p>The indwelling nephrostomy catheter market has seen steady growth in recent years and is projected to continue expanding at a CAGR of 5.6% during the forecast period.
