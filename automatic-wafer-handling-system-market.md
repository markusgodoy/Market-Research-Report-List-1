<p><p>The automatic wafer handling system market includes two types: quartz boat and graphite boat. Quartz boat is a container made of high-purity quartz and is used for transporting and handling wafers in semiconductor manufacturing processes. It offers excellent purity and thermal stability. On the other hand, the graphite boat is also used for carrying and manipulating wafers, but it is made of graphite material. Graphite boat provides good thermal resistance and high mechanical strength, making it suitable for handling wafers in harsh environments.</p></p>
