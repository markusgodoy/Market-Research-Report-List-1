<p><p>The Natural Gas Heavy Truck market consists of two main types: LNG Heavy Truck and CNG Heavy Truck. LNG Heavy Trucks run on liquefied natural gas, which is cooled and compressed. These trucks have a longer driving range and are commonly used for long-haul transportation. On the other hand, CNG Heavy Trucks use compressed natural gas, which is stored at high pressure. These trucks are typically used for shorter distances and are more suitable for local delivery or urban transportation. Both types aim to provide cleaner and more sustainable alternatives to traditional diesel-fueled trucks.</p></p>
