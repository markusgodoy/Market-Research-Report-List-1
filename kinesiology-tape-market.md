<p><p>Kinesiology tape is available in two main types: roll type and precut type. Roll type kinesiology tape is a continuous strip of tape that can be cut into the desired length and shape before application. It provides more flexibility and customization options for different body parts and injuries. Precut type kinesiology tape comes in pre-cut shapes and sizes specifically designed for common muscle and joint areas, making it quick and convenient to apply without the need for cutting. Both types serve the purpose of providing support, reducing pain, and promoting proper movement and healing in sports and rehabilitation.</p></p>
