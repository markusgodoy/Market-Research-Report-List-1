<p><p>The Laser Marking Services market comprises various types, including CO2, YAG, and others. CO2 laser marking services use carbon dioxide gas to emit a laser beam, providing high-power output and versatility suitable for plastics, wood, glass, and more. YAG laser marking services, on the other hand, employ yttrium-aluminum-garnet crystals, offering high precision and durability for metals, plastics, ceramics, and similar materials. The "others" category encompasses alternative laser technologies like fiber lasers, UV lasers, and green lasers, each with unique advantages and applications in the laser marking industry.</p></p>
