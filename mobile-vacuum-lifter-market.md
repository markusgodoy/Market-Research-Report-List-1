<p><p>The mobile vacuum lifter market comprises two types: electric vacuum lifters and pneumatic vacuum lifters. Electric vacuum lifters are powered by electricity and utilize an electric motor to create suction for lifting objects. They offer higher lifting capacity and are suitable for indoor use. On the other hand, pneumatic vacuum lifters are powered by compressed air and use a pneumatic system to create suction. They are generally lighter and more portable, making them suitable for outdoor applications. Both types provide efficient and convenient solutions for lifting and moving objects.</p></p>
