<p><p>The Genetic Analyzer market comprises several types of technologies including DNA Microarrays, PCR, Next Generation Sequencing (NGS), Serial Analysis of Gene Expression (SAGE), and Northern Blotting. DNA Microarrays enable studying gene expression on a genomic scale. PCR amplifies specific DNA sequences for analysis. NGS allows rapid sequencing of large DNA samples. SAGE quantifies gene expression through the sequencing of short tags. Lastly, Northern Blotting detects specific RNA molecules for gene expression analysis. All these technologies are crucial in understanding genetic information and studying gene expression patterns.</p></p>
