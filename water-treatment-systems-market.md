<p><p>Water treatment systems market includes various types such as filtration systems, water softeners, distillation systems, disinfection, and others. Filtration systems purify water by removing impurities and contaminants through a physical or chemical process. Water softeners remove minerals like calcium and magnesium that cause hardness in water. Distillation systems purify water by boiling it and collecting the condensed vapor. Disinfection systems kill or inactivate harmful bacteria and microorganisms in water. Other types may include ion exchange systems, reverse osmosis systems, and ultraviolet sterilizers, among others.</p></p>
