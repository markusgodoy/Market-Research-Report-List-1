<p><strong>The Orthopedic Joint Replacement Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Total Replacement</li><li>Partial Replacement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Orthopedic Joint Replacement Market includes two main types: Total Replacement and Partial Replacement. Total Replacement refers to the complete removal of the damaged joint and its replacement with a prosthetic implant. It is often performed when the entire joint is severely affected by conditions such as arthritis. On the other hand, Partial Replacement involves replacing only the damaged portion of the joint with an implant while preserving the healthy parts. This procedure is commonly used when a specific portion of the joint, such as the hip or knee, is affected.</p></p>
