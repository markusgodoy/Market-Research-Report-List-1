<p><p>Health Diaphragm Valves market types include manual, pneumatic, electric, and other types. Manual diaphragm valves are operated manually by turning a handwheel or lever to control the flow. Pneumatic diaphragm valves use compressed air to actuate the valve, allowing for remote operation. Electric diaphragm valves are controlled by an electric actuator that opens and closes the valve electronically. Other types include hydraulic diaphragm valves, which use hydraulic pressure for operation. These different types offer varied benefits and are chosen based on the specific application and requirements in the health industry.</p></p>
