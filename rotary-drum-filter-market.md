<p><p>The Rotary Drum Filter market consists of two main types: Vacuum Rotary Drum Filter and Non-vacuum Rotary Drum Filter. Vacuum Rotary Drum Filters use suction to remove water and solids from the filter, making it suitable for industries requiring drier and cleaner filtrates. On the other hand, Non-vacuum Rotary Drum Filters rely on gravity or pressure to separate solids and liquids, making it a cost-effective option for industries handling a large volume of fluids. Both types cater to different filtration needs depending on the desired level of filtration and industry requirements.</p></p>
