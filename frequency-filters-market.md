<p><p>Frequency filters are electronic devices used to selectively block or allow specific frequencies in a signal. There are two main market types for frequency filters: passive and active. Passive frequency filters consist of only passive components such as resistors, capacitors, and inductors. They do not require an external power source and are used for simple filtering applications. On the other hand, active frequency filters use active components like transistors or operational amplifiers to provide gain and can be used in more complex filtering applications.</p></p>
