<p><strong>The Medium Radar System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CW Radars</li><li>Pulsed Radars</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medium radar system market consists of two main types: CW radars and pulsed radars. CW radars, also known as continuous wave radars, emit a continuous radio frequency signal without interruption, enabling them to measure the velocity of moving objects accurately. Pulsed radars, on the other hand, emit short bursts of radio frequency signals and then listen for the echo, allowing them to determine the range and location of targets accurately. Both types have their unique advantages and applications within various industries such as defense, aerospace, and automotive.</p></p>
