<p><strong>The Medical Antifungal Agent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Azoles</li><li>Echinocandins</li><li>Polyenes</li><li>Allylamines</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical antifungal agent market can be categorized into several types. Azoles are a common type that inhibits the growth of fungal cells. Echinocandins are another type that targets the fungal cell wall, preventing their reproduction. Polyenes are antifungal agents that bind to the cell membrane of fungi, causing them to lose their functionality. Allylamines are a type that inhibits a specific enzyme in the fungal cell, impairing their growth. Lastly, there are other miscellaneous antifungal agents with different mechanisms of action.</p></p>
