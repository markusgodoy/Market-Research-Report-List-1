<p><p>Clean Room Robot market comprises various types of robots, including articulated robots, SCARA robots, parallel robots, Cartesian robots, and collaborative robots. Articulated robots are designed with multiple joints allowing flexibility in movement. SCARA robots are known for their fast and precise vertical and horizontal movements. Parallel robots use a system of parallel linkages for high-speed and high-precision operations. Cartesian robots move along three linear axes and are suitable for handling heavy loads. Collaborative robots work alongside humans, with advanced safety features and ease of use. Each robot type caters to different requirements in clean room environments.</p></p>
