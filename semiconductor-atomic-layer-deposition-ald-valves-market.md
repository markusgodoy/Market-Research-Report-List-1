<p><p>The semiconductor atomic layer deposition (ALD) valves market includes two types of valves: pneumatic actuated and electric actuated. Pneumatic actuated valves are driven by compressed air, which causes the valve to open or close. They are known for their reliability, high speed, and ability to handle high-pressure applications. On the other hand, electric actuated valves are operated by an electric motor, providing precise control over the valve's movement. They are favored for their quieter operation, energy efficiency, and suitability for automation. Both types play a crucial role in controlling the flow of gases during ALD processes in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
