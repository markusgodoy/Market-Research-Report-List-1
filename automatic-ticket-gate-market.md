<p><p>The automatic ticket gate market consists of various types of gates used for controlling access in high-traffic areas. Swing gates are single-panel gates that swing open and close in one direction. Full panel gates are vertical gates that open and close horizontally, allowing passage for one person at a time. Tripod turnstiles are three-arm gates that rotate to allow one person through at a time. Bi-parting gates consist of two panels that slide apart to permit passage. These gates are commonly used in transportation hubs, stadiums, and public buildings to ensure efficient and secure access control.</p></p>
