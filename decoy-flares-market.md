<p><p>The Decoy Flares market consists of three main types: Magnesium/Teflon/Viton (MTV) Decoy Flares, Spectral Decoy Flares, and Radio Frequency Decoy Flares. MTV Decoy Flares are composed of a mixture of magnesium, teflon, and viton, providing effective countermeasures against infrared-homing missiles. Spectral Decoy Flares release a broad spectrum of wavelengths to confuse and bypass heat-seeking missiles. Radio Frequency Decoy Flares utilize radio frequency emissions to distract radar-guided missiles. These types of decoy flares serve as reliable defense mechanisms used by military forces worldwide to divert and deceive enemy weapons.</p></p>
