<p><p>A Lightning Protection System (LPS) refers to measures taken to protect buildings and structures from the damaging effects of lightning strikes. There are two main types of LPS markets: Conventional and Non-Conventional systems. Conventional LPS utilizes traditional methods like air terminals, down conductors, and grounding systems to redirect lightning strikes. On the other hand, Non-Conventional LPS employs modern technologies such as lightning rods, surge protectors, and conductive coatings to provide comprehensive protection against lightning strikes. Both these market types aim to minimize the risk of property damage and ensure the safety of occupants during lightning storms.</p></p>
