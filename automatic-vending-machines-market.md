<p><p>Automatic vending machines market can be categorized into three main types based on their size: small size, medium size, and large size. Small size vending machines are compact and portable, capable of dispensing a limited range of products, such as snacks and beverages. They are commonly found in office spaces and small retail stores. Medium size vending machines are larger in size and have a higher capacity for products, offering a wider variety of items, including snacks, drinks, and even cosmetics. They are usually installed in larger retail outlets, shopping malls, and public spaces. Large size vending machines are spacious and can accommodate a diverse range of products, such as fresh food, hot drinks, and even electronic devices. They are often found in busy public areas like airports, train stations, and multiplexes.</p></p>
