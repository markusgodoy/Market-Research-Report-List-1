<p><p>Transfer switches are devices used to switch electrical power supply between two sources. There are two main types of transfer switches: automatic and manual. Automatic transfer switches are equipped with sensors that can detect power outages and instantly switch to an alternative power source. This type requires no human intervention and is commonly used in critical facilities like hospitals. Manual transfer switches, on the other hand, require human intervention to switch power sources. This type is typically used in residential settings or smaller commercial establishments where power outages are less critical.</p></p>
