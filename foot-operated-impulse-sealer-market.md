<p><p>The Foot Operated Impulse Sealer market includes two types: Single Impulse and Double Impulse sealers. Single Impulse sealers are commonly used for low to medium volume sealing applications. They have a single heating element that quickly heats up, creating a seal in one swift process. On the other hand, Double Impulse sealers are ideal for high volume sealing purposes. They have two heating elements, which generate a stronger and more secure seal by applying heat in two stages, ensuring better sealing efficiency and durability. Both types cater to different sealing needs based on the required volume.</p></p>
