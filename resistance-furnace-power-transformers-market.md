<p><p>Resistance furnace power transformers are used to supply power to resistance heating furnaces, commonly used in industrial applications. There are two types of market classifications based on voltage regulation. Direct voltage regulation transformers have a built-in voltage regulator that adjusts the output voltage based on the load, ensuring a constant voltage supply to the furnace. On the other hand, indirect voltage regulation transformers rely on external voltage regulators to control the output voltage. Both types of transformers play a crucial role in maintaining the performance and efficiency of resistance heating furnaces.</p></p>
