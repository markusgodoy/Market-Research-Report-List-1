<p><p>A Cardiovascular Information System (CVIS) is a software solution that helps in the management of patient data related to cardiovascular procedures. The CVIS market is divided into two types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based CVIS involves storing and accessing data on remote servers via the internet, offering flexibility and scalability. On-premise CVIS, on the other hand, involves hosting the software and data within the organization's infrastructure. While cloud-based offers cost-effectiveness and ease of access, on-premise provides more control over data security and customization options.</p></p>
