<p><p>In the K-12 market, cloud computing is categorized into three types: SaaS (Software as a Service), IaaS (Infrastructure as a Service), and PaaS (Platform as a Service). SaaS provides educational software applications and tools accessible through the internet, allowing schools to easily manage and utilize them without the need for local installations. IaaS offers virtualized infrastructure systems, such as servers and storage, enabling K-12 institutions to scale resources on-demand. PaaS provides a platform for application development and deployment, facilitating schools in creating and customizing their own educational software solutions.</p></p>
