<p><p>The heated swimming pool equipment market can be divided into two types: indirect heating equipment and direct heating equipment. Indirect heating equipment refers to systems that use heat exchangers, such as boilers or heat pumps, to warm the pool water. These systems are more energy-efficient and have lower upfront costs. On the other hand, direct heating equipment involves methods like solar panels or electric heaters directly heating the pool water. These systems may be more environmentally friendly but can have higher operating costs.</p></p>
