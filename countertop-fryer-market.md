Additionally, the rising trend of home-cooked meals and the growing inclination towards restaurant-style cooking at home are further boosting the market's growth.</p><p>Furthermore, advancements in technology have led to the development of innovative features in countertop fryers, such as digital controls, adjustable temperature settings, and built-in safety mechanisms. These features enhance the overall cooking experience and contribute to the market's growth.</p><p>With regards to market trends, there is a growing preference for countertop fryers with larger capacities, allowing users to fry larger quantities of food at once. <p><p>Countertop fryers are available in two main types: electric and gas. Electric countertop fryers are powered by electricity and are known for their convenience and ease of use. They are often preferred for smaller kitchens or locations where gas lines might not be accessible. On the other hand, gas countertop fryers use natural gas or propane as an energy source. They are chosen for their faster heating and cooking capabilities, making them suitable for high-volume cooking in commercial settings.</p></p>
