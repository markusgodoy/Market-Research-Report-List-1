<p><p>Flexible Screen Market Types:</p><p>PMOLED (Passive-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diode) is a type of flexible screen that uses a simpler technology where a grid of control transistors regulates the flow of current to the organic pixels. It offers lower production costs and simpler designs, suitable for smaller panel sizes and limited color applications.</p><p>AMOLED (Active-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diode) is a more advanced flexible screen technology that uses thin-film transistors to control each pixel individually, providing higher resolution, faster response times, and better color reproduction. It is well-suited for larger panel sizes, full-color displays, and offers enhanced visual quality and flexibility in design.</p></p>
