<p><p>The PC case market is divided into two main types: vertical cases and horizontal cases. On the other hand, horizontal cases are intended to lie flat on a surface, providing a wide and stable platform for the PC components. Horizontal cases are often favored for their ease of access and can accommodate larger and bulkier components.
