<p><strong>The Food And Beverage Nano-Enabled Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Packaging</li><li>Intelligent Packaging</li><li>Controlled Packaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Active packaging involves the incorporation of active compounds into packaging materials to actively interact with the food or beverage product, such as oxygen scavengers or antimicrobial agents. Intelligent packaging includes features like time-temperature indicators or RFID tags to provide information on the product's quality and condition. Controlled packaging refers to packaging materials engineered to control specific properties of the food or beverage product, such as moisture or gas permeability. These types of nano-enabled packaging aim to enhance product safety, quality, and shelf-life.</p></p>
