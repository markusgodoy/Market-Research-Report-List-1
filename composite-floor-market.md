<p><p>The composite floor market consists of three types: High Pressure Laminated Flooring, Solid Wood Composite Floor, and PVC Composite Floor. High Pressure Laminated Flooring refers to laminated boards made by compressing multiple layers of materials like resin, fiberboard, and decorative paper under high pressure. Solid Wood Composite Floor is composed of a solid wood top layer adhered to a plywood or fiberboard base layer. PVC Composite Floor is made of a blend of PVC and other materials, providing durability and a stylish appearance.</p></p>
