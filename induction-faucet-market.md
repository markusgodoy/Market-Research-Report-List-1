<p><p>The induction faucet market consists of two main types: infrared sensor faucets and touch sensor faucets. Infrared sensor faucets use infrared technology to detect the presence of a user's hands and automatically turn on the water flow. This touchless operation promotes hygiene and water conservation. On the other hand, touch sensor faucets require physical contact with the faucet to activate the water flow. These faucets usually have a touch-sensitive surface that responds to the user's touch, providing a convenient and modern option for users.</p></p>
