<p><ul><li>Evacuated Tube Collector</li><li>Flat Plate Collector</li><li>Unglazed Water Collector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The residential solar water heaters market offers different types of collectors such as evacuated tube collectors, flat plate collectors, and unglazed water collectors. Evacuated tube collectors use a series of glass tubes to absorb sunlight and transfer heat to the water, while flat plate collectors consist of a dark plate that absorbs solar energy and transfers it to the water flowing through pipes. Unglazed water collectors are mainly used in warmer climates and do not have a glass covering, allowing direct contact between sunlight and water to heat it.</p></p>
