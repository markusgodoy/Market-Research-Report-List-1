<p><p>Endoscope carts refer to mobile equipment used for storing and transporting endoscopes in medical facilities. Tower endoscope carts are designed vertically, providing a compact and organized storage solution for multiple endoscopes. They often include features like a charging system and a built-in sink for easy cleaning. On the other hand, endoscope storage carts are horizontal and used for temporary storage during procedures. Both types of carts play a crucial role in maintaining the efficiency and hygiene of endoscopy departments.</p></p>
