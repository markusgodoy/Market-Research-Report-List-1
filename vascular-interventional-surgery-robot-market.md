<p><p>The Vascular Interventional Surgery Robot market consists of two types: Image Navigation and Magnetic Navigation. Image Navigation robots rely on advanced imaging technologies to guide surgeons during vascular interventions. They provide real-time visual feedback, enhancing precision and accuracy. On the other hand, Magnetic Navigation robots employ magnetic fields to control instruments remotely, allowing for minimally invasive procedures in hard-to-reach areas. Both types of robots offer innovative solutions for vascular surgeries, improving patient outcomes and reducing risks associated with traditional surgical methods.</p></p>
