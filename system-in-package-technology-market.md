<p><p>System-in-Package (SiP) technology encompasses various types of packaging options for integrated circuits (IC). 2-D IC packaging involves mounting and interconnecting ICs on a single silicon substrate. In 2.5-D IC packaging, multiple ICs are stacked vertically on an interposer, offering higher integration and performance. 3-D IC packaging involves stacking multiple IC layers vertically, enabling improved performance, reduced size, and power consumption. Multifunctional Substrate Integrated Component Package combines components like ICs, passive devices, and interconnects on a single substrate, reducing size and improving system integration. These technologies enhance the performance and miniaturization of electronic devices.</p></p>
