<p><p>Portable spectrometers come in two main types: chip type and modular type. Chip type spectrometers are small, compact devices that integrate all the necessary components, such as light sources, detectors, and electronics, on a single chip. They are highly portable and suitable for applications where size and weight are critical factors. On the other hand, modular type spectrometers are larger and more flexible, allowing users to interchange different components for customized measurements. They offer higher performance and versatility but are slightly less portable compared to chip type spectrometers.</p></p>
