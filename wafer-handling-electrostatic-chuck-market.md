<p><strong>The Wafer Handling Electrostatic Chuck Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type Electrostatic Chuck</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type Electrostatic Chuck</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wafer handling electrostatic chuck market comprises two main types: Coulomb Type Electrostatic Chuck and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type Electrostatic Chuck. Coulomb Type Electrostatic Chuck operates based on Coulomb forces, which use an electric field to hold the wafer in place. On the other hand, Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type Electrostatic Chuck utilizes hydraulic pressure to restrict the wafer's movement. Both types essentially function to securely hold wafers during processing, ensuring precise and accurate handling to enhance overall manufacturing efficiency.</p></p>
