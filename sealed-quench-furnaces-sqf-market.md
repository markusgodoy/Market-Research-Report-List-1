<p><ul><li>Straight Through Type</li><li>In-Out Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sealed Quench Furnaces (SQF) are used for heat treatment processes in various industries. There are two types of SQF markets: Straight Through Type and In-Out Type. Straight Through Type SQFs have a linear design where the material enters from one end and exits from the other. This type is suitable for high-volume production. In-Out Type SQFs have a circular design and the material enters and exits through the same opening. This type is ideal for low to medium volume production and offers better energy efficiency and temperature uniformity.</p></p>
